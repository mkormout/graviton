  <how-to-verify>
Run the game in Godot editor (F5 or Play button). Press Enter to trigger the first wave (3 Suiciders).

**Test 1 — Locked vector charge + dodge window (D-01, D-02):**
1. When the wave spawns, observe Suiciders approaching. They should lock their heading when they first detect you.
2. Move your ship sideways (perpendicular to their approach). They should NOT track your new position — they steer toward where you WERE.
3. Observe acceleration: they should start slower and get faster as they close range (thrust ramp D-02).

**Test 2 — Re-acquisition on miss (D-03):**
1. Dodge a Suicider so it flies past your old position.
2. It should slow, turn, and re-lock onto your CURRENT position for a second approach.
3. It should NOT fly offscreen forever.

**Test 3 — Contact detonation (D-04, D-07, D-08):**
1. Let a Suicider reach your ship (don't dodge one).
2. On contact: explosion should trigger (particles, audio, light), player should take damage AND be visibly pushed by knockback.
3. Check console: should see "[EnemyShip] state: IDLING -> SEEKING" and "[WaveManager] Enemy died".

**Test 4 — Shot-to-death detonation:**
1. Shoot a Suicider before it reaches you.
2. On death: same explosion should trigger at the Suicider's position.
3. If you're within radius (~450 units), you should feel the knockback.

**Test 5 — No double explosion (D-06):**
1. In any test above, verify only ONE explosion per Suicider death.
2. No duplicate particle effects, no double damage.

**Test 6 — No loot (D-12):**
1. After killing Suiciders, no coins or ammo should drop.

**If explosion does NOT damage/push the player:** Check console for warnings. The `hit_ships = true` export on suicider-explosion.tscn should make the Explosion Area2D detect the PlayerShip. If not working, report the console output.
